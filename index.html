Introduction

Inspired by Jerome McGann and Lisa Samuels' Deformance and Interpretation, this project is a collection of programmatic experiments to three texts: The Box-Social by James Reaney, The Story of An Hour, by Kate Chopin, and The Yellow Wallpaper, by Charlotte Perkins Gilman.
